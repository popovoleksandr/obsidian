pūjārthāsanam arghyādi- 
sthāpanaṁ vighna-vāraṇam
śrī-gurv-ādi-natir bhūta- 
śuddhiḥ prāṇa-viśodhanam

pūjā—worship; artha—for the purpose; āsanam—a seat; arghyādi—beginning with arghya; sthāpanam—placing; vighna-vāraṇam—protection from obstacles; śrī-guru—with the spiritual master; ādi—beginning; natiḥ—offering obeisances; bhūta-śuddhiḥ—bhūta-śuddhi; prāṇa-viśodhanam—purifying the life breath.
