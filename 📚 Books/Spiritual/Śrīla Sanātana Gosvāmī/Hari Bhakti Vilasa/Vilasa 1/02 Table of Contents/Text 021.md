naivedya-bhakṣaṇaṁ santaḥ 
sat-saṅgo 'sad-asaṅgatiḥ
asad-gatir vaiṣṇavopa- 
hāsa-nindādi-duṣkalam

naivedya-bhakṣaṇam—eating food offered to the Lord; santaḥ—the devotees of the Lord; sat-saṅgaḥ—association with the devotees of the Lord; asad-asaṅgatiḥ—avoiding the association of non-devotees; asad-gatiḥ—the result of associating with non-devotees; vaiṣṇava—of the devotees; upahāsa—mocking; nindā—offenses; ādi—beginning with; duṣkalam— the inauspicious result.
